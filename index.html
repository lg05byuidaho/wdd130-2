Jeremie | Grand'Anse | Haiti

Jérémie is located in the Jérémie Arrondissement in Haiti and is the main city of the Grand'Anse region. It was founded in 1756. Navassa Island is located around 40 nautical miles to the west of the city. It is the centre of economics, culture and politics of Grand'Anse.
